# KPI Definitions

| KPI | Definition |
|---|---|
| Total Sales | Sum of all sales values |
| Total Profit | Sum of all profit values |
| Profit Margin | Total Profit / Total Sales × 100 |
| Total Orders | Distinct count of Order ID |
| Total Customers | Distinct count of Customer ID |
| Average Order Value | Total Sales / Total Orders |
| Units Sold | Sum of Quantity |
| Monthly Growth | Percentage change in monthly sales |
